# Code of Conduct

We want this project to be a welcoming, productive place to collaborate.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being respectful and constructive
- Assuming good intent and asking clarifying questions
- Accepting feedback gracefully
- Focusing on whatâ€™s best for users and the project

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, personal attacks, or discrimination
- Unwanted sexual attention or sexualized language
- Threats of violence or encouragement of harm
- Publishing private information (doxxing)

## Scope

This applies to all project spaces: issues, pull requests, discussions, and other official communication channels.

## Enforcement

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it via the SECURITY policy process.
Maintainers may take any action they deem appropriate, including content removal or contributor bans.
